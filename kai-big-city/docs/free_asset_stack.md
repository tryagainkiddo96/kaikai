# Free Asset Stack

This project should stay aggressively low-cost and use free assets first.

## Character

- Find a dog or canine base mesh that can be reshaped into a Shiba-like silhouette.
- Use a stylized or low-poly look instead of realism.
- Prioritize readable shape, tail curl, ears, and animation charm over detail count.

## Environment

- Use stylized city and neighborhood props.
- Build a compact block with:
  - sidewalks
  - fences
  - alleys
  - storefront props
  - benches
  - small rooftop details

## Animation

- Prefer free animation sources and retargeting instead of custom animation from scratch.
- Focus first on:
  - idle
  - walk
  - trot
  - sprint / zoomies
  - jump
  - bark
  - sniff
  - interact / paw

## Recommended Sources

- Quaternius for free CC0 model packs and animation-friendly packs
- Poly Pizza for individual props and stylized city pieces
- Kenney for general-purpose free game assets and UI support
- Mixamo for free character animation workflows and retargeting references

## Art Direction Rules

- Keep the palette warm and readable
- Favor charm and motion over realism
- Use lighting to sell time of day and mood
- Keep the city slightly exaggerated so Kai reads clearly at all times
