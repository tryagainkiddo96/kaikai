# Prototype Scope

## Must Have

- Playable Shiba controller
- Camera that feels good at street level
- Sprint
- Jump
- Bark
- Sniff vision or highlight mode
- Interact prompt
- Collectible treat system
- Lightweight quest tracker
- Small neighborhood map

## Nice To Have

- Wearable cosmetic item
- Pushable objects
- NPC reaction barks / speech bubbles
- Hidden rooftop route
- Dig spot interaction

## Not For First Slice

- Full open world
- Complex combat
- Vehicles
- Large dialogue systems
- Multiplayer
- Huge mission chains

## Success Condition

The prototype is successful if a player can spend 10 to 15 minutes in the level, find multiple routes, collect items, complete a tiny task, and come away liking Kai.
