# Kai Big City

`Kai Big City` is a cozy exploration game where you play as Kai, a mischievous Shiba Inu exploring a dense neighborhood full of shortcuts, snacks, secrets, and tiny adventures.

The goal is to build a small, polished, personality-driven game inspired by the feel of `Little Kitty, Big City`, but centered on dog movement, playful interactions, and neighborhood exploration.

## Pillars

- Be a lovable troublemaker.
- Explore a compact but layered city block.
- Collect treats, trinkets, and wearable items.
- Solve light environmental problems through dog behavior.
- Keep the tone playful, charming, and readable for a solo project.

## Prototype Goals

- Third-person Shiba character controller
- Small street / alley / park playground
- Bark, sprint, jump, sniff, and interact actions
- Collectible treats and simple quest items
- Friendly NPC interactions
- One clear short-term goal and one hidden collectible route
